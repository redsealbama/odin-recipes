Thor's Famous Lasagna Recipe

Description from Thor himself

You're sitting there with a group of friends on a Saturday night and you can choose to order a pizza or you can choose to make something everyone will love. With the recipe here, you will be sure to impress. Almost everyone loves a hot, cheesy lasagna. Layered with roasted red peppers, beef and ricotta, this lasagna will fill up the whole family and leave delicious leftovers for tomorrow. Feeds multiple people and they will always come back for the mouth-watering and savory lasagna that you cook.

Ingredients

  • 1(16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef 85/15
  • Salt/pepper & other seasonings to taste
  • 1(16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 pound shredded chessar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until soft; drain.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  3. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper; drain. Stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer 5 minutes.
  4. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, Cheddar and ricotta; stir well.
  5. In desired pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled.
  6. Bake (covered) in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
